Harsha Vardhan Simhadri

Principal Researcher, Microsoft Research India

Interests
I work on algorithmic research with a view towards future platforms and practical systems. A few examples:

  • Efficient algorithms for web-scale nearest-neighbor search that are now mainstreamed in Bing Ads and search (DiskANN)
  • New ML operators and architectures for tiny IoT and edge devices (EdgeML)
  • Parallel algorithms and run-times with provable guarantees for multi-core processors (PhD thesis)
  • New algorithms for topic models and word embeddings.
